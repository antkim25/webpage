Rick Astley's Never Gonna Give You Up

(yes, I am very interested in this song actually)

Rick Astley's "Never Gonna Give You Up" was released on July 27th in 1987.

It is considered to be his signature song.

Some nice facts about the song:

  1. About the song:
    • Rick Astley won the MTV Europe Music Award for Best Act Ever in 2008 with this song.
    • He recorded and released a pianoforte version of this song in 2019.
    • The song itself is 3 minutes 35 seconds long on the album version.
    • It was recorded in October of 1986, at PWL Studios in South London, England.
    • Its baselines were produced using a Yamaha DX7 digital synthesizer.
    • A Linn 9000 was used for the drums and sequencing.
    • The youtube video reached 1 billion views on July 28th of 2021, 34 years and 1 day after the release of the song.
  2. About the meme:
    • Its first appearance was in "It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia", which grew the song's popularity on the internet.
    • Then the creator of 4chan created a word filter that changed the word "egg to duck".
    • Then, someone made a post about an eggroll, which was changed to duckroll, which became a small meme.
    • People made hyperlinks that you would click on, expecting something different, which led you to the duckroll meme image.
    • The first trailer for Grand Theft Auto IV came out.
    • People made fake hyperlinks to this, which actually sent people to the Never Gonna Give You Up music video.
    • This started rickrolling, and the meme got bigger, even helping the music video get to 1 billion views!
